What Does Recycle Mean?

"Recycle" is the last of the three R's — it involves processing used materials into new products to reduce waste and conserve resources. Recycling helps keep valuable materials out of landfills and reduces the need for new raw materials and manufacturing energy.

But how can we recycle? We can start by properly sorting our waste, ensuring plastics, paper, and metals go into recycling bins instead of landfills. We can also support initiatives that turn recycled materials into new products, creating a circular economy.
