OUR UPCOMING PROJECTS

Our current project aims to connect as many organizations as possible in Bali. With this, the implementation of the 3 R's, clean-ups, and conservations can be done efficiently, leading us to a sustainable future with green lands and blue oceans.

The 3 R's Initiative

Reduce, Reuse, and Recycle initiatives to minimize waste and promote sustainable living across Bali.

Ocean Clean-Ups

Regular clean-up campaigns to remove plastic and pollutants from Bali's beaches and marine ecosystems.

Marine Conservation

Protecting coral reefs and marine life through awareness and active conservation efforts.

Organization Network

Connecting local and international organizations to amplify our environmental impact across Bali.
