WHAT WE DO

We aim to create efficient 3R (Reduce, Reuse, Recycle) initiatives, cleanups, and conservation efforts — connecting organizations and empowering Bali’s blue future.

PROGRAMS & PROJECTS

Organization Visitation

Each month, we visit one or more environmental organizations to exchange insights, updates, and strengthen community partnerships.

Spreading Volunteers

We connect volunteers with partner organizations to help with cleanups, conservation projects, and other activities that make a real impact.

Communication Media

Our team spreads awareness through media campaigns that encourage responsible waste management and environmental care across Bali.

Educational Articles

We create informative content to educate the community about marine conservation, inspiring everyone to take part in restoring Bali’s ocean.
