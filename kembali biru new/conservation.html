What Does Conservation Mean?

"Conservation" focuses on preserving marine ecosystems and restoring ocean health. In Bali, organizations work tirelessly to protect coral reefs, mangroves, and endangered marine species. Conservation ensures that future generations can continue to benefit from a thriving, biodiverse environment.

Through education, research, and hands-on action, conservation initiatives tackle the root causes of environmental degradation. From coral restoration to mangrove rehabilitation, every effort contributes to maintaining the delicate balance of our oceans.

The organizations below are dedicated to protecting Bali's marine life and habitats. Together, they contribute to a more sustainable and balanced planet for all living creatures.
